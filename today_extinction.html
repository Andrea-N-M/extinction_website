Thylacine

The Thylacine also known as the Tasmanian tiger went extinct on 7 September 1936. Thhey were led to extinction becuase of over hunting, disease, and habitat destruction. Learn more about the Thylacine.

Pinta Giant Tortoise

The last Pinta Giant Tortoise, named George died in captivity on June 24, 2012. Deforestation and using them as a food source for whales led them to extinction. Learn more about the Pinta Giant Tortoise and recently extinct animals

Maui Akepa

This native Maui bird was listed as possibly extinct in 2018. The last sighting of the bird was in 1988. The main theory of their extinction is due to the avian flu because of mosquitos. Learn more about the Maui Akepa and recently extinct animals
